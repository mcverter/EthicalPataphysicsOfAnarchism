Certain doublings are opposed to each other

Totality and Infinity narrates the recurrent cyclings of these doubles. Different sections of the book invert or bend these double relations. In one section, the Self occupies a place of height looking at the depth of the destitute other. In another section, the Other occupies a place of height looking down at the subject self in hypostasis and substitution. Other sections apply other metaphorical curvature tensors to warp the space furter
