Combinations

Prefixes, suffixes, prepositions, etymological roots, and verbal, nominal, adjectival and adverbial combinations all belong to various THEMATIC_GENRES. This enables a single word to envelop an N-ply manifold of THEMATIC_GENRES.

For example, the word "survivor" belongs to the genre GENESIS because the etymological root is "vivre". It belongs to the genre MATHEMATICS because the prefix "sur" connotes height.

The birth of the son is referred to as a "resurrection" because it again rises (surgir) again (re), thus combining "height" and "recurrence" from MATHEMATICS
