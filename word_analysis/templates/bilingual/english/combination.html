Combinations

Prefixes, suffixes, prepositions, etymological roots, and verbal, nominal, adjectival and adverbial combinations all belong to various THEMATIC_GROUPS. This enables a single word to envelop an N-ply manifold of THEMATIC_GROUPS.

For example, the word "survivor" belongs to the group GENESIS_GT because the etymological root is "vivre". It belongs to the group MATHEMATICS_GT because the prefix "sur" connotes height.

The birth of the son is referred to as a "resurrection" because it again rises (surgir) again (re), thus combining "height" and "recurrence" from MATHEMATICS_GT
