Combinations

Prefixes, suffixes, prepositions, etymological roots, and verbal, nominal, adjectival and adverbial combinations all belong to various THEMATIC_GROUPS. This enables a single word to envelop an N-ply manifold of THEMATIC_GROUPS. For example, the word "survivor" belongs to the group GENESIS_GT because the etymological root is "vivre". It belongs to the group MATHEMATICS_GT because the prefix "sur" connotes height. conjugations, noun declensions, and etymological roots all are Combinations (survivre = sur (height) + vivre (Family Group)
